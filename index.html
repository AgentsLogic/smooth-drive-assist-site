Frequently asked questions

What vehicles are supported?
We support 250+ vehicle models from Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, and many more. Check our supported vehicles page for the full list.
How long does installation take?
Most installations take 2-4 hours. We'll give you a more accurate estimate when you book based on your specific vehicle.
Is this legal?
Yes. openpilot is a driver-assist system, not autonomous driving. You remain responsible for the vehicle at all times, just like with factory ADAS systems.
What's included in the $3,000?
Everything: the comma 3X device with openpilot pre-installed, your vehicle-specific harness, professional installation, calibration, testing, and a hands-on training session.
Why the 2-week lead time?
We need to order the correct harness for your specific vehicle. Each harness is vehicle-specific and takes time to arrive.
Do you offer financing?
Currently we require a $1,500 deposit upfront with the remaining $1,500 due at drop-off. We're exploring financing options for the future.
What if I sell my car?
The device can be transferred to another compatible vehicle. We offer re-installation services for a fee.
