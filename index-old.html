How Smooth Drive Assist works

A clear, safety-first process from compatibility check to support.

1. Compatibility check & planning

We review your vehicle, driving patterns, and goals. You learn what the driver-assist system can and cannot do, and we confirm whether your car is a good candidate before booking an install.

2. Professional system install on your vehicle

Our technicians install and wire the driver-assist system on your vehicle without defeating required safety equipment. We keep everything tidy, secure, and serviceable.

3. Calibration & test drive

We calibrate sensors and complete a controlled test drive, verifying stable operation under real road conditions around Austin.

4. Training & ongoing support

You leave with hands-on training, clear safety guidelines, and access to optional support plans for updates, diagnostics, and future recalibration.

What to expect on install day

We make install day as simple as possible: you drop your vehicle off, we handle the install and testing, and we bring your car back with the system installed and verified.

  • Drop off your vehicle & settle the balance. You arrive at your scheduled time, review the plan with us, and pay the remaining $1,500 for your install. The $1,500 deposit you already paid is applied toward your $3,000 total.
  • Ride home on us. After drop-off, we'll arrange an Uber or give you a ride back home or to work within the Austin metro area so you're not stuck waiting at the shop.
  • Professional install & initial calibration. Our technicians install the system on your vehicle, route and secure the harness, and perform the initial calibration and safety checks.
  • 50–100 mile shakedown drive. We test drive your vehicle for roughly 50–100 miles under normal Austin driving conditions to confirm calibration, stability, and system behavior before we return it to you.
  • Delivery, training & handoff. Once everything checks out, we deliver your vehicle back to you with the system installed, walk you through how it works, and go over safety guidelines and limitations. You drive away in full control, with the system ready to assist.

Plan on your vehicle being with us for most of the day. We'll confirm the exact timing and logistics when we schedule your install.

Frequently asked questions

Is this "self-driving"?

No. These systems provide advanced driver assistance – they help with steering and speed in certain conditions, but you must remain attentive and ready to take over at all times. You are always the driver.

Where do you operate?

We are currently focused on the Austin, Texas metro area. Driver-assist systems are installed on your vehicle at our local facility by appointment only.

What vehicles are compatible?

Compatibility depends on make, model, year, and condition. Start by checking our supported vehicles list, then share your vehicle details in the booking form. We confirm compatibility before taking your deposit and scheduling a driver-assist system install on your vehicle.

Why do you require a $1,500 deposit?

The deposit lets us order the correct harness and parts for your specific vehicle and reserve shop time for your install. The $1,500 deposit is applied toward your total install cost. We confirm compatibility before sending a deposit link, so you are not asked to pay a deposit if your vehicle is not a good fit.

What happens if something goes wrong?

We design driver-assist system installs on your vehicle to be serviceable and reversible. If you experience issues, our support plan customers receive priority diagnostics, and we work to resolve problems promptly and safely.
