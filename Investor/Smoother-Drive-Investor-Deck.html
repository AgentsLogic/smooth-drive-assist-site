1. Business Overview

SmootherDrive is a mobile AI driver-assist installation business based in Austin, Texas. The company sells and installs an AI-powered driver assistance device compatible with 325+ vehicle makes and models. The device runs OpenPilot, rated the #1 driver assistance system by Consumer Reports, providing hands-free lane keeping, adaptive cruise control, and automated highway lane changes — without requiring the purchase of a new vehicle.

The business operates with zero fixed overhead. All hardware is sourced directly through the manufacturer with free shipping. The operator, John Graham Jr, handles sales, scheduling, and installations. The business accepts payment via PayPal (claimman@mail.com) and the website at smootherdrive.com.

5.1 Investment Amount

The Investor agrees to provide a one-time seed investment of $10,000 (ten thousand dollars) to the Operator upon signing this Agreement. Funds are to be delivered via PayPal to claimman@mail.com or by mutually agreed method.

5.2 Nature of Agreement

This Agreement is a Revenue Participation Agreement and does not convey any equity ownership, voting rights, board representation, or claim on business assets. The Investor is not a partner, shareholder, or officer of SmootherDrive. The Investor has no liability for business debts or obligations.

5.3 Payment Terms

Phase 1 — Return of Principal: Beginning the first full calendar month after funds are received, the Operator shall pay the Investor 10% of monthly net profit until the Investor has received a cumulative total of $10,000. At projected volumes (~2 pro installs/week), this is expected to occur within approximately 4 months.

Phase 2 — Ongoing Profit Share: After the Investor's full $10,000 principal has been returned, the Operator shall pay the Investor 5% of monthly net profit on an ongoing basis for the remaining term of this Agreement.

Payments shall be made on or before the 15th of each calendar month for the prior month's net profit. Net profit is defined as gross revenue less cost of hardware, harness, and technician labor. Overhead-free by design — no rent, no salaried staff costs are deducted.
