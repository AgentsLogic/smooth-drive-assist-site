Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Smoother Drive

What vehicles are supported?
We support 325+ vehicle models from Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, and many more. Check our supported vehicles page for details.
How long does installation take?
Most installations take 2-4 hours depending on your specific vehicle model and configuration.
Is this legal and safe?
Yes. Smoother Drive is a driver-assist system, not autonomous driving. You remain fully responsible for the vehicle at all times, with the system providing assistance only.
What's included in the installation?
Everything you need: the Smoother Drive device with software pre-installed, your vehicle-specific harness, professional installation, system calibration, comprehensive testing, hands-on training, and 30-day support.
Do I need a monthly subscription?
No. Our pricing is a one-time cost of $2,000 (launch special). Optional support plans are available for $100/month but are not required.
How do software updates work?
Smoother Drive receives regular over-the-air updates that add new features and improvements. Updates are free and optional - you control when to install them.
