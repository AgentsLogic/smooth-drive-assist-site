## What We Do

We take an AI driving device — the same technology behind Tesla Autopilot, but open-source and compatible with 325+ cars people already own — and we sell and install it for them.

The device handles **lane centering, adaptive cruise control, and stop-and-go traffic automatically.** The driver stays fully in control. It just makes highway driving dramatically less exhausting.

**No new car needed. No $40,000 Tesla. Just your car, upgraded.**

We sell two ways:

- **Ship it to you (DIY):** $2,100 — device + wiring harness + step-by-step guide, delivered to your door
- **We install it for you:** $3,000 — our team handles everything in Austin, Houston, Dallas, Fort Worth, San Antonio, or Corpus Christi
